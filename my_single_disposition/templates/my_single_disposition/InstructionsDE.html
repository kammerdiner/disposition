Disposition Game

In the Disposition game, you will attempt to maximize profits while buying and selling a series of stocks and avoiding the Disposition Effect.

Gameplay

Each round consists of the time it takes for the bar at the top of the game to run out. At the end of that timer, a new round is started. During a round, a player can buy or sell exactly 1 stock. Your decision is not submitted until the end of the round. You may undo and decision to buy or sell until the end of the round.

Next to each stock you will see a series of values:

  • Market Fluctuation is how much the market price has changed since the last round.
  • Market Price is the current value of the stock if it were bought or sold.
  • Acquisition Price is the price you purchased or short-sold the stock at last.
  • Owned is the current amount of units the player owns of the stock.
